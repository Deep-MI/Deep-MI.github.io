Collaborations, Visits & Students

We welcome Humboldt Fellowship postdoctoral stays (6–24 months), master thesis projects, and academic/industry visitors. If you are interested in a visit or collaboration, please reach out by email. We currently do not have open funded positions.

As a member of the Humboldt Foundation Network, we are particularly happy to host researchers via a Humboldt Postdoctoral Fellowship.

Directions

DZNE Bonn Campus

By public transportation

  • Take Bus 601 (e.g. from Bonn central station) to the endpoint “Uniklinikum Süd”
  • Walk straight, left at the roundabout — after 100 m the DZNE building is on your right (green/orange panels)
  • Talk to the receptionist and someone will pick you up

By car

  • Enter Uniklinikum, keep right at the first roundabout, park at Parkhaus Süd
  • Walk left — the DZNE building is on the right

Martinos Center, Charlestown

By public transportation

  • Take the Red Line to Charles/MGH
  • Walk through MGH main campus to the shuttle bus stop
  • Take the Charlestown shuttle to the last stop (Building 149)
  • Check in with reception (photo ID required)

By car

  • Parking garage next to Building 149
  • Cross 13th Street, enter Building 149
  • Check in with reception (photo ID required)
