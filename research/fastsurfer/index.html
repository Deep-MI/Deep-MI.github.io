One pipeline, whole-brain output

FastSurfer combines an advanced neural-network segmentation (FastSurferCNN / VINN) with a fast surface pipeline (recon-surf) for volumes, cortical surfaces, thickness and parcellations.

CerebNet — cerebellum

Integrated module for fast, reliable and detailed cerebellum sub-segmentation.

HypVINN — hypothalamus

Integrated module for automated sub-segmentation of the hypothalamus and adjacent structures.

FastSurfer-CC — corpus callosum

Automated morphometry of the corpus callosum and its sub-regions, derived from the whole-brain segmentation.

FastSurfer LIT — lesion inpainting

Lesion inpainting module enabling robust surface reconstruction in brains with tumors or cavities.
